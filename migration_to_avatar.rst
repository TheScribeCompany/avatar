3. Migration to Avatar
======================

Migration to Avatar involves moving from legacy identity management systems to a decentralized ecosystem. 
This shift is directed by the growing demand for enhanced privacy, security, and interoperability. 
Avatar achieves this by leveraging technologies like including blockchain and cryptographic techniques for secure authentication.
